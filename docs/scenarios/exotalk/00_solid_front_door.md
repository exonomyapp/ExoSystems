# Scenario 00: The Solid Front Door

## Narrative Context
Isabella isn't just another UN Special Rapporteur drafting polite condemnations from a quiet office in Geneva; she's an "Indiana Jane" of international law, operating deep in the field. Currently embedded in East Jerusalem, she is tracking and documenting the systematic destruction of civilian infrastructure. But her relentless fieldwork has drawn the eye of the technocratic empire. 

Her centralized communications—WhatsApp, Gmail, even secure organizational endpoints—have been compromised, flagged, and throttled by deep-packet inspection and advanced Palantir predictive algorithms. The algorithms have identified her as a high-risk "person of interest," tracking her metadata, isolating her contacts, and freezing her out of the very institutional networks meant to protect her. A shadow ban has turned into a total digital siege. 

Just as her credentials die and her screens flash 'ACCESS DENIED', a trusted government insider manages to slip past the blockade, sending her a cryptographic "dead drop"—a raw, decentralized link to the ExoTalk binary. With searchlights crossing the dark sky and the hum of diesel generators echoing through the city, she launches the app on her isolated, vintage terminal.

## Cognitive Shift
**Centralized Expectation**: "I need to log in with my email and password so the central server knows who I am, authenticates my access, and grants me permission to speak."
**Decentralized Reality**: "The app is a gateway. I don't need a server to exist. My identity is forged locally, cryptographic and self-determined. Autonomy isn't given by an institution; it's claimed by the individual."

## World Event Reference
The atmosphere is heavy with the realities of asymmetric warfare. The local power grid in the Levant is flickering, subjected to "tactical load shedding." Internet service providers are actively throttling encrypted traffic during ongoing "security operations," attempting to force all comms onto easily monitorable, unencrypted channels. Isabella's digital struggle is a microcosm of the broader physical blackout happening around her.

## Technical Milestones
- First-time launch of the ExoTalk binary on a clean, isolated system.
- Visual inspection of the "Non-Elastic" UI frame, emphasizing local control over cloud synchronization.
- Zero network traffic emission until the local identity is explicitly authorized and established.
