# Scenario 08: The Enterprise Relay

## Narrative Context
**Amnesty International** adopts the ExoTalk/Conscia stack for its field workers in the Andes. They deploy a fleet of "Enterprise Relays"—nodes that handle high-volume document sync while maintaining 100% local sovereignty. No **Palantir** mapping, no **World Bank** metadata harvest.

## Cognitive Shift
**Centralized Expectation**: "Sovereignty is only for individuals; organizations need centralized IT."
**Sovereign Reality**: "Sovereignty scales. The Enterprise Relay provides organizational coordination without organizational exposure."

## World Event Reference
The targeting of **Amnesty International** and **Doctors Without Borders** staff by state-sponsored spyware in Latin America and Asia.

## Technical Milestones
- Deploying a "Multi-Tenant" Conscia node.
- Managing multiple "Sovereign Identities" on a single relay.
- Verifying high-throughput document synchronization.
