# Scenario 09: The Independent Echo

## Narrative Context
Isabella broadcasts her final report—a definitive archive of the war crimes she witnessed. The report doesn't "live" on a server; it echoes across the global mesh. It is mirrored in Hangzhou, Beirut, the Andes, and countless personal vaults. The truth is now unassailable.

## Cognitive Shift
**Centralized Expectation**: "I’ll post it on social media and hope it doesn't get taken down."
**Decentralized Reality**: "The truth is a permanent blob on the mesh. De-platforming is impossible."

## World Event Reference
The systematic deletion of civilian-recorded footage from centralized social media platforms under pressure from state actors.

## Technical Milestones
- Global "Broadcast" of a large data blob.
- Verifying 10:1 replication across the federated mesh.
- Demonstrating "Undeletability" via local vault persistence.
