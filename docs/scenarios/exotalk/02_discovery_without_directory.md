# Scenario 02: Discovery without Directory

## Narrative Context
Isabella needs to find **The Insider**. In a centralized world, she would search a global directory. But the national backbone is being "throttled" by state security. She uses ExoTalk’s gossip discovery. Through a tactical connection (mDNS/Gossip), her node "sees" the Insider's node nearby on the mesh.

## Cognitive Shift
**Centralized Expectation**: "I’ll search for him on the server."
**Decentralized Reality**: "We find each other through the mesh. No central index required."

## World Event Reference
**Amnesty International** reports on the "National Intranet" being used to isolate citizens during periods of civil unrest.

## Technical Milestones
- Activating the Gossip overlay.
- Real-time peer discovery on the local network.
- Visualizing the "Status Pulse" of a nearby peer.
