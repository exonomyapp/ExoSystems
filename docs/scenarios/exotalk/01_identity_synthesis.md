# Scenario 01: Identity Synthesis

## Narrative Context
Isabella is inside the app. She realizes there is no "Login" button. She clicks "Create New Identity" and sees an empty vault. She is at the birth of her sovereign presence. No **IMF** financial ID, no **World Bank** digital certificate—just her and the math.

## Cognitive Shift
**Centralized Expectation**: "The server will assign me a username."
**Sovereign Reality**: "I synthesize my own keys. My `did:peer` is my passport, calculated locally."

## World Event Reference
The **World Bank**'s push for "Digital ID" in emerging markets as a prerequisite for aid—the ultimate tool of financial colonialism.

## Technical Milestones
- Clicking "Synthesize" to generate Ed25519 keys.
- Assigning a local display name ("Isabella").
- Clicking "Initialize Identity" to commit the vault.
- Verifying the DID via the Telemetry API.
